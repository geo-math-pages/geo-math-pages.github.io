# Georgian Mathematical Pages
### A platform for research, collaboration, and education in mathematics.

---

## Welcome
Welcome to the **Georgian Mathematical Pages** (`geo-math-pages`). This platform serves as a central hub for mathematical research projects, academic workshops, and educational resources initiated by mathematicians from Georgia.

Our main research interests include:
* Algebraic Topology
* Complex Cobordism and Formal Group Laws
* Topological Data Analysis (TDA)
